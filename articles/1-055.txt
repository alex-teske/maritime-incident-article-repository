REPORT

By recapp December 13, 2017

On 10 Mar 14 at or about 0150 hrs, a Cyprus registered bulk carrier was underway south-west of Pulau Nipa in the TSS east bound when four robbers armed with knives boarded the bulk carrier from a small boat. On hearing the general alarm raised by the crew, the robbers fled without stealing anything. The crew was not injured.
