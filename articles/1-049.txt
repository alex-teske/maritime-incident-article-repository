REPORT

By recapp December 13, 2017

At about 0600 hrs on 15 May 15, Malaysia-registered product tanker Oriental Glory departed Labuan, East Malaysia for Tanjung Manis, Sarawak, East Malaysia when six fishing boats surrounded Oriental Glory at 03° 34.50’ N, 110° 39.08’ E (approximately 60 nm northwest of Bruit Island, Malaysia). About 30 perpetrators boarded Oriental Glory and took the tanker to another location at 05° 22.02’ N, 109° 33.06’ E (approximately 188 nm northwest of Bruit Island, Malaysia) and siphoned off 2,500 MT of ship fuel/oil. All crew was safe and the vessel is proceeding to Tanjung Manis for further investigation.
